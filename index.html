City of the Day: Herat

Herat is one of the most historically and culturally significant cities in Afghanistan. It has long been a center of art, poetry, architecture, and intellectual life. During the Timurid Empire, Herat flourished as a major cultural capital.

The city is renowned for Persian literature, traditional calligraphy, and miniature painting. Herat’s cultural identity reflects centuries of artistic excellence and craftsmanship.
